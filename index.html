Landingpage Essentials (Checklist)
  • Clear H1 with value prop
  • Primary CTA above the fold (action language)
  • Support proof: logos, quotes, stats
  • Focused form (min fields), privacy hint near CTA
  • Sticky or repeated CTA
  • Trust (stars, guarantees, contact options)
  • SEO hygiene: title, meta desc, canonical, one H1
